Octal, Hex, Binary and Horse

Which number system would a horse use?

This module I enjoyed learning about numbering systems. Some of the ones we covered include:

  • Binary
  • Octal
  • Hex

It sure seems like horses would use binary because they have two hooves in front and could only count to two. Unless, of course, they used all four hooves and then they would have a 0-3 based numbering system. Horses are pretty logical though, and would probably see the appeal of binary.

The real question is: Would they balk at convention and start counting with 1 instead of zero?

It is also important to mention that horses love computers. They have historically enjoyed the soft whir of computer fans, their ever increasing battery life, and their ability to store and play music.

And with that I will leave you with this totally true quote:

Horses have always loved computers
-Benjamin Franklin
